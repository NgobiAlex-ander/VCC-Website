Overview

At Victory City Church Rubaga, we are committed to spreading the gospel and serving those in need. Our mission teams travel locally and internationally, bringing hope and support to various communities. Whether it's through building homes, providing medical care, or sharing the message of Christ, we are dedicated to making a positive impact.

Current Missions

We are currently engaged in several mission projects, including:

  • Building Victory Ciy churches branches in Agago,Mbarara and Jinja.
  • Spreading the gospel through community outreach programs.
