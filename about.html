Our Vision and Leadership

Victory City Church is committed to building a community of believers deeply rooted in God's word, passionate about worship, and actively engaged in transforming our city through love and service. We strive to reflect God's kingdom in all its diversity and power, providing a nurturing environment for spiritual growth and empowerment.

Pastor Moses Nsubuga

Senior Pastor

"Our vision is to build a community of believers who are deeply rooted in God's word, passionate about worship, and actively engaged in transforming our city through love and service."

Pastor Treasure Lwantale

Assistant Pastor

"I envision Victory City Church as a place of healing and growth, where every member is equipped to use their God-given gifts and make a lasting impact in their spheres of influence."

Elder Kahigwa

Church Elder

"Our vision is to foster a culture of discipleship, where mature believers mentor the younger generation, creating a continuous cycle of spiritual growth and leadership development."

Elder Nyanzi Philip

Church Elder

"We aim to expand our outreach programs, addressing both spiritual and practical needs of those around us, demonstrating God's love in tangible ways."
